## 🤝 How to earn bonus credits (totally optional)

You already have Ultra Plan. This part is if you want extra credits:

### 1. Build something real with CodeFlicker
Not "Hello World" — something you actually want to make.

### 2. Write about your experience
~1,000 words, or a 3-10 min video, covering:
- What you built
- What you liked
- What needs work
- What surprised you

**You don't need:**
- ❌ Professional blog quality
- ❌ Marketing BS
- ❌ Clickbait titles

**Just write like you're talking to a friend.** If it's all praise, we'll think an AI wrote it 😂

### 3. Post it online
Reddit, Dev.to, Medium, your blog, YouTube — pick one.

### 4. Drop the link in Discord
Post it in the angel users channel, any format works:

```

```

**That's it.**

## 🤝 What We Need from You (It's Simple)

Simple and fair exchange:

### Step 1: Build Something Real
Use CodeFlicker to create an actual project (not just "Hello World")

### Step 2: Share Your Experience  
Write a short article including:
- What you built
- What you liked (be specific!)
- What could be better (honest feedback welcome)
- One thing that surprised you

### Step 3: Post It Online
Share on **any one** of these platforms:
- Reddit (r/buildinpublic, r/webdev, etc.)
- Dev.to
- Medium
- Your personal blog
- X (Twitter)

### Step 4: Submit the Link
Post your article link in our Discord `#angel-showcase` channel

**That's it!** No catch, no pressure to go viral. Use this format:

```
📝 Post Completed!
Platform: Reddit r/buildinpublic
Project: Automated backup script
Link: https://reddit.com/xxx
```

## 🎯 How It Works

### Phase 1: Apply (2 minutes)
Fill out [this short form](#) telling us what you want to build

**We're looking for:**
- Real developers who want to build something specific
- People willing to share their honest experience

### Phase 2: Get Approved (1-2 days)
We'll review your application and send you:
- ✅ Approval email with 10,000 credits
- 📚 Quick start guide
- 🔗 Discord invite link

### Phase 3: Build (Your Pace)
Take your time. No deadline. Build something awesome.

**Stuck?** Ask in Discord - we're here to help!

### Phase 4: Share (30-60 minutes)
Write about your experience. We **don't need**:
- ❌ Marketing fluff
- ❌ Viral posts
- ❌ Professional blog quality

We **do need**:
- ✅ Honest feedback (good and bad)
- ✅ Specific examples
- ✅ Your authentic voice

### Phase 5: Earn More (Optional)
Quality content gets rewarded:
- **Basic post** (meets requirements): +1,000 credits
- **Quality content** (detailed, helpful): +2,000-3,000 credits
- **High engagement** (20+ likes/comments): +3,000-5,000 credits
- **Viral hit** (100+ upvotes): +5,000-10,000 credits

Keep building and sharing = keep earning credits!

## 📜 Official Program Rules

### Eligibility Requirements

✅ **You must have:**
- GitHub account (created >3 months ago with actual code)
- Real email address
- Willingness to share your experience publicly

✅ **Open to:**
- Students, hobbyists, professionals - all levels welcome
- Any country (but posts should be in English for wider reach)

❌ **Not eligible:**
- Brand new GitHub accounts with no activity
- People unwilling to share their experience
- Resellers or spammers

---

### Credit Usage Policy

**Credits can be used for:**
- ✅ Building your projects with CodeFlicker AI assistance
- ✅ Experimenting with features
- ✅ Learning how AI coding works

**Credits CANNOT be used for:**
- ❌ Reselling or sharing with others
- ❌ Commercial projects for clients (use your own credits)
- ❌ Abusive or automated usage

**One account per person.** Multiple applications from the same person will be rejected.

## QA

**Common Scenarios**

Q: What if I get the credits but don't post?  
A: That's okay! We won't take back the credits. But you won't get more, and future applications won't be approved.

Q: What if my post gets removed by moderators?  
A: Try posting to a different platform or adjust your content. Send us a message and we'll help you figure it out.

Q: What if I can't finish my project?  
A: No problem! We understand. Your credits stay valid. But to get bonus credits, we need a post about your experience (even if the project isn't 100% done).

Q: Can I post about multiple projects?  
A: Absolutely! Each quality post earns more credits.

Q: What if I want to quit the program?  
A: You can leave anytime. Your credits remain valid. No hard feelings!

---

**Top 5 Questions**

Q: Are the credits enough?  
A: For 1-2 medium projects, yes. If not, we'll add more after you post. We won't let you get stuck halfway through a project.

Q: Do I need to write professionally?  
A: Nope. 500-1000 words, write like you're chatting with a friend. Check the example above.

Q: What if my post gets no engagement?  
A: That's fine. We don't require virality. As long as it's honest, you'll get the base credits.

Q: Can I criticize the product?  
A: Absolutely! Real criticism is more valuable than fake praise. If you only say nice things, we'll suspect it's AI-written 😂

Q: What if I can't finish the project?  
A: Credits aren't taken back. But to get the bonus, at least write about why you stopped and what problems you faced - that's valuable feedback too.
